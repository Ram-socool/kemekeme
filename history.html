School History

San Roque Experimental High School was established during the school year 1966-1967 through the efforts of PTA Resolutions, Barrio Council Resolution No. 3 and 4, Municipal Resolution No. 86 and in compliance with General Memorandum No. 86 s. 1966.

School records show that on average, 350 pupils graduated from the elementary schools of Bamban, Tarlac each year. However, many of these graduates did not continue their high school education due to lack of time and the high cost of studying in private schools, which their parents could not afford. The Bureau of Public Schools, conscious of such existing problems besetting the children and parents and cognizant of the role of education in nation building, promulgated a set of guidelines for barrio high schools to solve these problems.

In a joint meeting of the Federation of PTA, Barrio Council, and Barrio Captain Association of Bamban, headed by Barangay Captain Armando Sibal, with the assistance of Mr. Bernardo Domingo Sr. and the duly elected President of PTA, Mr. Eduardo Y. Navarro, a resolution was filed requesting the honorable director of public schools, through the efforts of Mr. Vicente Lumibao, the Division Superintendent of Schools of Tarlac, for the opening of the Experimental Barrio High School in Barangay San Roque, Bamban, Tarlac, in accordance with the provisions of Memorandum No. 14 s, 1966. Mr. Fortunato Tiglao, the Principal of Bamban Elementary School, was tasked with organizing and managing the opening of classes in the said school.

San Roque Experimental High School later became San Roque Barangay High School and is now called San Roque National High School.

In 2011, the Department of Education (DepEd) administered a shift to a fresh learning scheme — the K to 12 basic education program that aims to enhance learners' basic skills, produce more competent citizens, and prepare graduates for lifelong learning and employment. "K" stands for Kindergarten and "12" refers to the succeeding 12 years of basic education (6 years of Elementary education, 4 years of Junior High School, and 2 years of Senior High School). San Roque National High School - Senior High School, has been established during the School Year 2016 - 2017.
