SPECIAL CURRICULAR PROGRAM

Admission

Admission (per RM 154 s. 2021)

To qualify in the ranking, interested learners must have met/possessed the following criteria, to wit:

  • Quarterly grade of 85% or higher in English, Science, and Mathematics and a grade of 83% or higher in the other subjects as evidenced by the grades of students up to third rating period of SY 2020 - 2021;
  • Good moral character; and
  • Preferably in good health and fit to undergo a rigorous academic program.

Documentary Requirements

  • Letter of Application/Intent
  • Certified true copy of school report card by the School Head
  • Certificate of good moral character signed by the School Head
  • Medical Certificate signed by a government physician.

STEPS in selecting the qualifiers shall be observed:

  • Online submission of report cards and other documentary requirements to the STE implementing schools shall be done on or before JUNE 18, 2021.
  • The screening process shall involve the evaluation of academic grades and an online interview.
  • The average grades in English, Science, and Math and Science shall comprise 90% of the overall grade of the applicant, while the 10% will be based on the online interview.
  • The applicants shall be informed of the schedule of the online interview by batch through text messaging or via email by the chosen school.
