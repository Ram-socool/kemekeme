Senior High School Curriculum

MaTaTag Curriculum

San Roque National High School proudly embraces the implementation of the MaTaTag Curriculum, in alignment with the standards set by the Department of Education (DepEd). This enhanced curriculum is designed to strengthen foundational learning, with a focused emphasis on Mathematics, Science, Technology, and Reading.

By integrating the MaTaTag Curriculum, our school is committed to equipping students with a solid academic base that supports lifelong learning and prepares them to meet future challenges with confidence. The program fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and the real-world application of knowledge, essential for success in today’s evolving society.

Our adoption of this curriculum reflects San Roque National High School’s unwavering dedication to academic excellence and its support for national educational reforms.
